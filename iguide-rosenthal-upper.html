🔥 BBQ (Nexgrill 5-Burner Propane)

Propane gas grill on the deck. Tank is connected underneath. Always open the lid before lighting.

1
Set all knobs to OFF, then open the lid
2
Slowly open the propane tank valve counter-clockwise. Wait 10 seconds.
3
Turn one burner knob to HI and press the electronic ignition button for 3–5 seconds until lit
4
Confirm the burner is lit. Repeat for additional burners. Close lid and preheat for 10 minutes.
5
Shutdown: turn all knobs to OFF, then close the propane valve clockwise. Side burner works the same way.
No foil on grates · Clean grates after use · Never leave unattended · Let us know if propane runs out
